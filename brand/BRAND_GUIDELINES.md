## âœï¸ Typography

### Font Stack

#### Heading Font: **Inter**
- **Usage**: All headers, titles, navigation, CTAs
- **Fallback**: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Segoe UI, Roboto, sans-serif
- **Weights**: 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 600 (semi-bold), 700 (bold)
- **Personality**: Clean, friendly, modern but not trendy

#### Body Font: **Source Sans Pro**
- **Usage**: All body text, descriptions, content
- **Fallback**: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Segoe UI, Roboto, sans-serif
- **Weights**: 300 (light), 400 (regular), 600 (semi-bold)
- **Personality**: Highly readable, warm, approachable

#### Accent Font: **Poppins** *(Use Sparingly)*
- **Usage**: Special callouts, branding moments
- **Fallback**: Inter, system-ui, sans-serif
- **Weights**: 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 600 (semi-bold)
- **Personality**: Friendly, rounded, slightly playful

### Typography Scale

| Element | Size | Line Height | Weight | Font |
|---------|------|-------------|--------|------|
| **H1** | 2.5rem (40px) | 1.2 | 600 | Inter |
| **H2** | 2rem (32px) | 1.3 | 600 | Inter |
| **H3** | 1.5rem (24px) | 1.4 | 500 | Inter |
| **H4** | 1.25rem (20px) | 1.4 | 500 | Inter |
| **Body Large** | 1.125rem (18px) | 1.6 | 400 | Source Sans Pro |
| **Body** | 1rem (16px) | 1.6 | 400 | Source Sans Pro |
| **Body Small** | 0.875rem (14px) | 1.5 | 400 | Source Sans Pro |
| **Caption** | 0.75rem (12px) | 1.4 | 400 | Source Sans Pro |

### Typography Guidelines
- **Minimum body text**: 16px for accessibility
- **Generous line spacing**: Improves readability and feels more relaxed
- **Hierarchy**: Use size, weight, and color to create clear content hierarchy
- **Breathing room**: Don't crowd textâ€”let it breathe

## ðŸ·ï¸ Logo & Watermark

### Main Logo
- **File**: `logo.png`
- **Contains**: Kiin wordmark + tagline "Care coordination made easier"
- **Usage**: Headers, official communications, presentations

### Watermark
- **File**: `watermark.png` 
- **Contains**: Kiin wordmark only, 60% opacity
- **Usage**: Video content, image attribution

### Logo Usage Rules
- **Clear space**: Maintain space around logo equal to the height of the "K"
- **Minimum size**: Never smaller than 100px wide
- **Never distort**: Always maintain original proportions
- **Background contrast**: Ensure logo is always readable

### Watermark Guidelines
- **Position**: Bottom-right corner by default
- **Size**: 5% of video/image width
- **Opacity**: 60% for videos, adjust as needed for images
- **Placement**: 20px margin from edges

## ðŸ—£ï¸ Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice: **Warm, Supportive, Authentic**

#### Personality Traits
- **Understanding**: "We know this is hard"
- **Practical**: "Here's how we can help"
- **Encouraging**: "You've got this"
- **Authentic**: Real experiences, honest challenges
- **Calm**: Reassuring presence in stressful times

#### Writing Style
- **Perspective**: "We understand because we care too"
- **Language level**: Clear and accessible, avoiding jargon
- **Sentence structure**: Short to medium sentences, active voice
- **Tone**: Conversational but knowledgeable

### Things to Avoid
- âŒ Clinical or medical jargon without explanation
- âŒ Corporate speak ("solutions," "leverage," "optimize")
- âŒ Overly cheerful or fake positivity
- âŒ Condescending language ("simply," "just," "easy")
- âŒ Cold, robotic tone

### Key Messages
- "You're not alone in this journey"
- "Small steps make a big difference"
- "Care for yourself too"
- "Every family's situation is unique"
- "Technology should make life easier, not harder"

### TTS Settings
- **Voice**: en-US-AriaNeural
- **Rate**: Slightly slower than normal (0.9x)
- **Tone**: Warm and conversational
- **Pauses**: Natural breathing room between concepts

## ðŸ“ Content Guidelines

### Content Principles
1. **Empathy first**: Acknowledge the emotional reality
2. **Practical value**: Every piece should help somehow
3. **Authentic voice**: Real experiences over generic advice
4. **Inclusive language**: Welcome all family structures and situations
5. **Accessible content**: Clear, simple, scannable

### Content Types & Branding

#### Social Media Posts
- Always include tagline in profile/bio
- Use brand colors for graphics
- Hashtags: #CareCoordination #CaregiverSupport #Kiin
- Tone: Encouraging, practical, community-focused

#### Video Content
- Apply watermark using `apply_branding.py`
- Use brand colors for any text overlays
- Include intro/outro when appropriate
- Closed captions for accessibility

#### Written Content
- Use brand fonts when possible
- Apply color hierarchy (headers in primary blue)
- Include helpful subheadings
- End with encouraging note

#### Email Communications
- Use warm background color
- Brand-consistent header
- Personal, conversational tone
- Clear next steps
