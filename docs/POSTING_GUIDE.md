## üîÑ Platform Differences & Adaptation

### Content Adaptation Matrix

| Element | TikTok | Instagram Reels | YouTube Shorts |
|---------|--------|----------------|----------------|
| **Video Length** | 15-30 seconds | 15-30 seconds | 30-60 seconds |
| **Caption Length** | 150-300 chars | 300-500 chars | 1000+ chars |
| **Hashtag Count** | 3-5 hashtags | 20-30 hashtags | Tags + description |
| **Music/Audio** | Trending sounds | Original + trending | Copyright-free preferred |
| **Text Overlay** | Minimal | Moderate | Detailed explanations |
| **CTA Style** | "Comment if..." | "Share your story..." | "Subscribe for more..." |

### Content Types by Platform

**TikTok Excels At**:
- Quick tips and hacks
- Emotional moments and realizations
- Trending challenges adapted for caregiving
- "Day in my life" content

**Instagram Reels Work Best For**:
- Aesthetic lifestyle content
- Behind-the-scenes caregiving
- Product reviews and recommendations
- Community building content

**YouTube Shorts Perfect For**:
- Educational deep-dives
- Step-by-step tutorials
- Resource compilations
- Long-form stories condensed

### Algorithm Differences

**TikTok Algorithm**:
- Favors completion rate and immediate engagement
- Values trending sounds and hashtags
- Rewards consistent posting (1-3x daily)
- Prioritizes recent content

**Instagram Algorithm**:
- Values saves and shares over likes
- Considers relationship with creator
- Favors content that keeps users on platform
- Rewards consistent engagement

**YouTube Algorithm**:
- Prioritizes watch time and click-through rate
- Values subscriber engagement
- Considers session duration
- Rewards long-term engagement patterns

## üîß Technical Best Practices

### Video Quality Standards:
- **Resolution**: Minimum 1080p (1920x1080)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16 vertical for all platforms
- **Frame Rate**: 24-30 fps
- **Audio**: Clear, no background noise
- **Lighting**: Natural or well-lit artificial

### Accessibility Requirements:
- **Captions**: Auto-generate + manual review
- **Text Overlay**: High contrast, readable fonts
- **Audio Description**: For visual-heavy content
- **Alternative Text**: For thumbnails and images

### Branding Consistency:
- **Color Palette**: Consistent across platforms
- **Font Choices**: Maximum 2 fonts per video
- **Logo Placement**: Subtle but consistent
- **Voice/Tone**: Matches community values

## üö® Common Pitfalls to Avoid

### Platform-Specific Mistakes:

**TikTok**:
- Using outdated trending sounds
- Over-editing (TikTok favors authentic content)
- Ignoring comments in first hour
- Posting irregularly

**Instagram**:
- Using wrong hashtags or banned hashtags
- Posting low-quality repurposed TikToks
- Ignoring Stories feature
- Not engaging with other accounts

**YouTube**:
- Clickbait titles that don't deliver
- Poor thumbnail design
- Inconsistent posting schedule
- Not optimizing for search

### Universal Mistakes:
- Posting without community context
- Ignoring platform-specific best practices
- Not adapting content for platform differences
- Focusing on vanity metrics over engagement quality
- Inconsistent brand voice across platforms
